Ingredients

  • Palak leaves
  • Spinach
  • Spices
  • Vegetables(tomatoes , onion , green chillies)
  • vinegar
  • salt
  • oil & butter
  • cashew nuts
  • garlic paste

Instructions

Step 1:Clean palak with water and spray vinegar and salt to it.Drain off water completely.

Step 2:Add oil and green chillies to the pan.

Step 3:Add palak and casheu nuts,saute until the palak wilts off completely.

Step 4:Cool it completely.Transfer these to a jar,pour 1/4 water to it and blend till u get thick consistency .

Step 5:In the another pan put butter,oil,spices,chopped onions.Fry the onions turn golden.Then add 3/4 teaspoon ginger garlic paste.

Step 6:Add the fine chopped tomatoes and salt.Later add required spices.Blend this mixture separately

Step 7:Add half teaspoon of Kasuri methi to it.Next add palak puree

Step 8:Add cubed paneer.
