PAKORA

Ingredients

  • gram flour
  • rice flour
  • Spices (ajwain)
  • Vegetables(carrot , onion , french beans , cabbage , bell pepper)

Instructions

Step 1: Boil and mash potatoes.

Step 2: Add 1 to 2 chopped chillies , 1 teaspoon ginger garlicpaste , 1/4 or 1/2 teaspoon garam masala , and half teaspoon salt.

Step 3:mix all these and squeeze to let the moizture from them . set aside for 10 minutes.

Step 4:add 12 cup gram flour , 1/4 teaspoon ajwain 1/2 cup rice flour.

Step 5: mix everything well . Don't mix too much water . dough must be stiff . check for salt , chillies .

Step 6:heat oil in pan on a medium heat . Take small portions and drop it in hot oil.

Step 7:fry until lightly gold.

Step 8:serve with chutney and chai.
